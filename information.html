Information on Food Waste

How much do we waste?

  • 40% of the food we are supposed to be eating is wasted
  • 1.3 billion tonnes of food goes in the trash
  • 20 billion pounds is wasted on farms every year

Why is food waste a problem?

  • $220 billion growing, transporting, and processing food that is wasted
  • Food waste costs $162 billion a year
  • Food waste in landfills create methane, a poisonous greenhouse gas.
  • Globally, methane gas from food waste makes up seven percent of total greenhouse emissions.
