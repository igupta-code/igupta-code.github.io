Where and how do we manage to waste so much food?

Food waste occurs at every one of the four steps of the food industry.
Production: Because of pest infections and severe weather, plants die before they can even be harvested.
Food processing: In storage, food is wasted because of pests. Especially in areas where it is hot or very humid.
Retail: The Natural Resources Defense Council did reaserch proving that the leading cause of food waste in America, was confusing expiration dates, such as best before, sell-by, or use-by dates. Retail stores throw away large amounts of food because of these confusions. If produce is misshapen, brused, or abnormal, they are thrown away even though they are completely edible. On top of this, 2.3 million tonnes of fish are discarded in the North Atlantic and Noth Sea every year because they are the wrong species or even just the wrong size.
