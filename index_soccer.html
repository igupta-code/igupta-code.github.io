Alex Morgan

Alex Morgan became the youngest member of the U.S. woman's national soccer team in 2009. She won her first Olymic god medal by helping the U.S defeat Japan at the 2012 Summer Olypics. Alex Morgan was born on July 2, 1989 in California, but didn't start playing soccer until she was 14 years old. Morgan attended the University of California at Berkeley, at the end of her college career, she was tied for thrird on the school's list for highest scores, with 45 goals. In March of 2016, Morgan and some other teammates filed a complaint against wage discrimation against U.S. Soccer, and the topic escalated when they filed a gender discrimination.
