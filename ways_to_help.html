Ways to help

How can you make a difference?

  • Buy and eat fruits that are considered “ugly” so they are not thrown away by the retail stores.
  • Use your common sense instead of the expiration date becasue there are many differenct kinds of expiration dates that are way too confusing to understand.
  • Store food correctly so that it doesn't spoil. Click here for information on expiration dates and storing food.

Don't Donnate Canned Food

What to donate: The best thing to donate to a food bank is money. No one really eats canned goods and they contain high amounts of sodium. Because food banks buy things at a wholesale level and have partnerships with farmers, they can buy a lot more food, with a lot less money. So with the dollar you spend on the can of beans,a food bank could have bought tons of fresh food that people will actually enjoy eating.
