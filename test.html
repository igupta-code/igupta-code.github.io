How do you use the Google Forms and Spreadsheet Below?

If you have a lack or a surplus amount of food, you can submit a response on the google forms below, telling the site what food you are in need off, what the expiration date of the food is, and any other important information. Once you have submitted a form, your answers will be uploaded to the Google spreadsheets below. Don't be surprised if your response doesn't show up immediately, it will take some time to load. Once your answers have been posted, other people or organizations will be able to see your post and respond to you using the contact information that you have provided.
